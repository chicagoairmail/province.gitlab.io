Date: 2019-06-06
Category: Nepal

Kathmandu district is one of the districts in Province 3. 
There are eleven municipalities in Kathmandu district: 

    Budanilkantha
    Chandragiri
    Dakshinkali
    Gokarneshwar
    Kageshwari Manohara
    Kathmandu
    Kirtipur
    Nagarjun
    Shankharapur
    Tarakeshwar
    Tokha
